Pancit Recipe

Description

Refers to various Filipino noodle dishes, with Pancit Canton and Pancit Bihon being popular variations. These dishes often include a mix of stir-fried noodles, meat (chicken, pork, or shrimp), and vegetables, seasoned with soy sauce and other flavorful ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Noodles (Canton or Bihon)
  • Meat (chicken, pork, or shrimp)
  • Vegetables (cabbage, carrots, green beans, etc.)
  • Soy sauce
  • Fish sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onions
