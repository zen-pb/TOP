Beef Caldereta Recipe

Description

Beef Caldereta is a savory Filipino stew known for its bold flavors. It typically features tender beef chunks stewed in a rich tomato-based sauce, with ingredients like liver spread, bell peppers, potatoes, carrots, and green olives. The dish is seasoned with a blend of spices, including garlic, onions, and sometimes chili peppers for a hint of heat. Beef Caldereta is a hearty and flavorful dish, often served during festive occasions and gatherings in Filipino households.

Ingredients

  • Beef chunks
  • Liver spread
  • Tomato sauce
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Bell peppers
  • Green olives
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Chili peppers (optional)
  • Fish sauce
