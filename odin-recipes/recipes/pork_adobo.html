Pork Adobo Recipe

Description

It's a savory dish where pork is stewed in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns until tender. It's known for its rich and flavorful sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pork pieces
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Peppercorns
