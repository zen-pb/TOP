Chicken Tinola Recipe

Description

A ginger-based soup with chicken, green papaya, and chili leaves. It's a light and comforting dish, commonly served with rice, and the broth is flavored with ginger and fish sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Fish sauce
  • Green papaya
  • Chili leaves
